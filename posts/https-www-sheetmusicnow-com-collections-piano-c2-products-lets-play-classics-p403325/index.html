The vocal cycle “Let’s Play Classics” presents modern view of composer David Tukhmanov and poet Yuriy Entin on unfading melodies by Mozart, Bach, Beethoven, Schubert, Chopin, Dvorák, Tchaikovsky and others. This unique musical project includes twelve pieces for children choir, which are arrangements of well-known compositions by classic composers.
